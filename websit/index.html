Overview

The Cisco Firepower 5506-X is a next-generation firewall (NGFW) that delivers advanced threat protection and enhanced network visibility. It is designed to secure small to medium-sized business environments with powerful security capabilities.

Specifications

  • Firewall Throughput: 750 Mbps
  • VPN Throughput: 100 Mbps
  • Interfaces: 8x Gigabit Ethernet
  • Max Concurrent Sessions: 20,000
  • SSL VPN Users: 50

Operation Mechanism

The Firepower 5506-X leverages stateful inspection, application visibility, and intrusion prevention to detect and block threats in real-time. It operates using Cisco's Firepower Threat Defense (FTD) software, providing integrated defense against sophisticated cyber threats.
