Pros

  • High performance in detecting advanced threats and attacks.
  • Easy management using the simple ASDM interface.
  • Supports advanced security policies and VPNs.
  • Strong integration with other Cisco products.
  • Includes advanced traffic analysis features.

Cons

  • Higher cost compared to some other devices in the market.
  • Requires good technical knowledge to configure complex settings.
  • Technical support can sometimes be slow in certain regions.
  • Device size might not be suitable for very small environments.
