Applications

The Firepower 5506-X is widely used in various environments to enhance network security and management:

  • Small to Medium Enterprises (SMEs) for perimeter security.
  • Data Centers for securing virtualized environments.
  • Educational institutions to protect student and faculty data.
  • Healthcare facilities to safeguard sensitive medical records.
  • Government sectors for advanced threat detection and compliance.
